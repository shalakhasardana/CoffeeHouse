ABOUT THE COFFEE

We don’t think we should be the only ones making it in the world. We want to see everyone succeed. That’s why we chose Caravan Coffee as the official roaster for Red Elm Cafe. Caravan is committed to ethically sourcing their coffee. That means the farmers that grow the beans we love are also making it in the world. “Every purchase of coffee meets one or more of the following certifications: Certified by Caravan’s Responsible Buying Analysis* Certified Fair-Trade coffee Organic Coffee certified by USDA Certified Rainforest Alliance *Caravan’s Responsible Buying Analysis looks at the Social and Environmental considerations of the purchase. We examine the traceability back to a specific farm or co-operative. Furthermore, we consider the sustainability to the farm workers including economic impact (e.g. fair price), safety, human rights and their environment.” – caravancoffee.com
